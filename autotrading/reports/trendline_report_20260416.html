Report Description

This report applies a deterministic trendline and support/resistance analysis across three timeframes for each selected ticker:

  • Short-term (15-min): 20 trading days, ~520 bars
  • Medium-term (1-hour): 60 trading days, ~420 bars
  • Long-term (daily): 260 trading days, ~260 bars

The methodology follows Grimes's pivot hierarchy, Edwards & Magee's trendline construction, and Kirkpatrick & Dahlquist's breakout confirmation framework, with Pring's volume divergence and Bulkowski's climax rules.

S/R zones: Support (gray) | Resistance (orange)

Dotted trendlines indicate a breakout has occurred from that point onwards. A separate per-ticker explanation file (one per timeframe) is saved alongside this report, walking through the step-by-step reasoning behind every trendline and signal.
